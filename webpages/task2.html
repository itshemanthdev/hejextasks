Ethonal Derivation

1. Hydrolysis of Ester:

CH3COOC2H5 + H2O ----------- H + -----------> CH3COOH + C2H5OH

2. Oxidation of Ethanol:

C2H5OH ----------- Oxidation -----------> CH3COOH

3. Formation of Calcium Acetate:

2CH3COOH + Ca(OH)2 ----------------------> (CH3COO)2Ca + 2H2O

4. Dry Distillation (Ketone Formation):

(CH3COO)2Ca -----------Dry distillation-----------> CH3COCH3 + CaCO3

FINAL PRODUCTS

* Ethanol (C₂H₅OH) is used.
* Acetic acid (CH₃COOH) is the key intermediate.
* Acetone (CH₃COCH₃) is the final product.

(CH3COO)2Ca ----------------------> CH3COCH3 + CaCO3
