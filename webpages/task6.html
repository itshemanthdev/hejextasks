Code & Conquer

-------------------------------

Silence deep, your fingers fly,

lines of code beneath the sky.

While others sleep, you chase the spark,

Debugging dreams into the dark.

A logic maze, a looping race,

Each error faced with patient grace.

You build the future, bit by bit,

sleepless nights, you never quit.

The world runs on what you create,

With every push, you shift the fate.

So hold your ground, refactor strong -

The road is tough, but you belong.

Remember this when bugs surround:

The greatest devs were once spellbound.
