Welcome to the School of Fly Birds

At the School of Fly Birds, we believe that every child has the potential to soar. Our mission is to provide a safe, nurturing, and intellectually stimulating environment where students are encouraged to explore, discover, and achieve excellence.

We combine strong academic foundations with a focus on creativity, leadership, and community values. From innovative classroom learning to hands-on projects and extracurricular activities, we ensure that every student receives a well-rounded education.

Join us in shaping future leaders, thinkers, and changemakers — ready to take flight in a rapidly evolving world.

About Us

The School of Fly Birds is a forward-thinking institution committed to providing quality education with a focus on holistic development. Founded in 2008, our school has grown into a vibrant learning community where students are inspired to reach their full potential.

Our dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and inclusive curriculum aim to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and values they need to thrive in a global society. We foster critical thinking, creativity, empathy, and leadership from the ground up.

Class Levels & Academic Groups

At Fly Birds, we offer structured academic and co-curricular programs tailored to each stage of a student's learning journey. All classes are available in both Tamil and English mediums.

Primary Classes (1st – 5th)

  • Foundational learning in languages, mathematics, environmental science, and moral education.
  • Fun-based activities including storytelling, drawing, singing, and games.
  • Introduction to computer literacy and physical development.

Middle & High School (6th – 10th)

  • Expanded curriculum with focused subjects: Science, Social Science, Mathematics, Languages, and Computer Applications.
  • Participation in intra- and inter-school competitions, Olympiads, and exhibitions.

Higher Secondary (11th & 12th)

Students choose from specialized academic streams based on their interests and career goals:

  • Computer Science Group: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Computer Science
  • Biology Group: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics
  • Commerce Group: Accountancy, Commerce, Economics, Business Maths
  • Humanities Group (Arts): History, Economics, Political Science, Sociology

In addition to academics, students continue engaging in leadership roles, club coordination, and project-based learning to prepare for higher education and beyond.
