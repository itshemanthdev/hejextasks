ABOUT

Nestled in a peaceful, health-conscious environment surrounded by lush greenery and gentle breezes, Hotel Heaven is your gateway to tranquility. Whether you're escaping the chaos of city life or seeking a rejuvenating retreat, our sanctuary offers the perfect blend of comfort, care, and calm.

Our clean and cozy rooms—available in both AC and Non-AC options—are thoughtfully designed to provide restful sleep and peaceful mornings. Wake up to the soft chirping of birds and the aroma of freshly brewed tea. Each room is equipped with essential amenities, ensuring a stay that’s both relaxing and convenient.

Indulge in wholesome, freshly prepared meals served three times a day, crafted with love and local ingredients. From hearty breakfasts to nourishing dinners, every bite reflects our commitment to health and taste. Complement your day with refreshing morning and evening tea, paired with delightful snacks that bring comfort and joy.

At Hotel Heaven, hospitality is not just a service—it’s a philosophy. Our warm and attentive staff are here to make your stay seamless and memorable. Whether you're lounging in our serene garden, meditating in quiet corners, or simply enjoying the stillness, every moment is curated to help you relax and recharge.

All this serenity comes at an incredibly affordable price—starting from just ₹7,999. So why wait? Step into a world where peace meets comfort, and let Hotel Heaven be your home away from home.

Where comfort meets care, and every moment feels like home. Welcome to Hotel Heaven!
